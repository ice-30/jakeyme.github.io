About

At Digital Leaders, we want to make a difference in IT at Kettlethorpe High School. We have made App-Books for subjects like Maths, Music, English and more. We want ICT to be used in every lesson in the school. It would be a great help to pupils and teachers. We have several proficiencies within the Digital Leaders team, including photomanipulation, web design and presentation construction. We aim to to change the way Kettlethorpe High School uses technology within the school. For example, using iPads and tablets in Art.
